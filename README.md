## 📌 Project Overview

The **Pizza Sales Power BI Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project that transforms raw pizza sales data into an interactive, insight-driven dashboard. Built using **SQL**, **Power BI**, and **DAX**, this project demonstrates a complete analytics workflow — from data extraction to strategic business insight generation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- 📊 Analyze overall pizza sales performance using key business KPIs
- 🏆 Identify top-performing and underperforming pizza products
- 📅 Uncover daily and monthly sales trends and peak demand periods
- 🔍 Enable data-driven decisions through dynamic filters and drill-downs

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- ✅ **Sales Trend Analysis** — Daily and monthly revenue and order patterns
- ✅ **Category & Size Breakdown** — Revenue split by pizza category and size
- ✅ **Top 5 & Bottom 5 Pizzas** — Ranked by revenue, quantity, and total orders
- ✅ **Dynamic Slicers & Filters** — Drill down by date range, category, and size
- ✅ **KPI Cards** — At-a-glance business performance metrics
- ✅ **Peak Period Identification** — Busiest days and months highlighted
